which of following could be unit digits of 57 ^ n, where n is a positive integer?
unit digit = 个位数 = rightmost digit
57 ^ n unit digit = 7 ^ n unit digit
57 ^ n = 7 ^ n  mod 10
since
57 ^ 2 = 3249, 7 ^ 2 = 49    
57 ^ 3 = 185193, 7 ^ 3 = 343 
57 ^ 4 = 10556001, 7 ^ 4 = 2401
so unit digit = 7, 9, 3, 1
